Sci-ModoM


a database of transcriptome-wide high-throughput RNA modification sites

Sci-ModoM v0.0.0-alpha is under active development: it's appearance and functionality may change at any time!

Sci-ModoM

, the central data repository and database hub for the Collaborative Research Centre RMaP, aims to

  • constitute a reference database for RNA modification high-throughput assays
  • integrate RNA processing events and other relevant public data
  • harmonize data exchange formats and interoperability standards
  • provide a critical assessment of and integration with public data
  • facilitate data curation, integration, access and availability
